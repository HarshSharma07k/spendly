Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login times and page visits, used to maintain and improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, and we do not link to your bank accounts or external financial services.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Operate and maintain your Spendly account.
  • Display your expense history and summaries back to you.
  • Send transactional emails (e.g., password resets) when you request them.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the Service.

We will never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon a verified deletion request, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate (for example, hosting infrastructure). These providers process data only on our behalf and are contractually bound to keep it confidential.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that profile individual users.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out through the contact details provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
